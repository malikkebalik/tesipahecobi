Terry Gerin (Rhino) Net Worth

What is Terry Gerin's Net Worth?

Terry Gerin is an American professional wrestler, politician, and actor who has a net worth of $4 million. Terry Gerin was born in Detroit, Michigan in October 1975. He is best known under his ring name Rhino. Gerin started out competing as an enhancement talent in WCW and the WWF during the 90s. He made a name for himself in Extreme Championship Wrestling before the company was bought out by the WWE.

Rhino competed for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) as well as Juggalo Championship Wrestling, Legend City Wrestling and Ring of Honor. He returned to the WWE working in NXT. Rhino is a three time WWF Hardcore Champion, a WCW United States Champion, and the inaugural WWE SmackDown Tag Team Champion with Heath Slater. He was a ECW World Heavyweight Champion and a two time ECW World Television Champion as well as an NWA World Heavyweight Champion in TNA. Gerin won the Republican Primary and is running for the Michigan House of Representatives in 2016.
