Who Was DMDK Vijayakanth?

Vijayakanth, a multifaceted personality, initially gained fame as a successful actor, producer, and director in the Tamil film industry. His contributions to cinema were noteworthy, and he garnered a massive fan following for his on-screen charisma. However, in a surprising move, Vijayakanth transitioned into politics, founding the DMDK and becoming a prominent political figure in Tamil Nadu.

Vijayakanth’s political journey saw him serve as a Member of the Legislative Assembly twice. He represented the constituencies of Virudhachalam and Rishivandiyam, showcasing his commitment to public service. His leadership in the DMDK marked a significant influence on the state’s political landscape.
