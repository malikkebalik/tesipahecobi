What is Magnus Uggla's Net Worth?

Magnus Uggla is a Swedish artist, composer, actor, and radio host who has a net worth of $12 million. Magnus Uggla was born in Stockholm, Sweden in June 1954. He was the lead singer of the rock band JUSO before he became a solo artist. Uggla has released 17 solo studio albums since 1975.

His first two albums sold less than 500 copies each in Sweden so he changed from glam rock to punk and hard rock and sold more than 150,000 copies on his next album. Many magazines nicknamed Uggla "The Swedish Ian Hunter". He also formed the Magnus Uggla Band. Uggla authored a book in 2008.
