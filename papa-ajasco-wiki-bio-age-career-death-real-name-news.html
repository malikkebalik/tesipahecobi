Who is Papa Ajasco? His Bio and Age

Papa Ajasco is a famous comedy sitcom of Nollywood that was created in 1996. The family sitcom has a character named Papa Ajasco and who was played by a Nigerian actor, Femi Ogunrombi. Femi was a professional actor who joined this comedy show in 2006, after the lead role, Abiodun Ayoyinka, who was playing Papa Ajasco’s character left the show. He made his acting so real that every lover of the show accepted him very fluently.

Femi was an ethnomusicologist, a music instructor, an actor, a singer, and a trained nurse. He was a multi-talented guy with lots of skills and has played tremendous roles in his life other than acting. His age has not been specified neither by him nor by any of his personal or official members.

Femi Ogunrombi was born in Nigeria in the Ife city of Osun state. The Ogunrombi family moved to Lagos in 1970 and so as Femi. Papa Ajasco’s Education

He attended a local school in Ife and after their shifting to Lagos, in 1975, he studied nursing, and then he did his training at a General Hospital, in Ikeja, Lagos. During his practicing time, he developed a musical interest and decided to join Obafemi Awolowo University in the 1980s, after which he studied Dramatic Arts from there. At the university, a choral group was founded and conducted by Femi which he named The Ayoro Voices. Between 1980 and 1983, that group became the cultural image of Obafemi Awolowo University.

Papa Ajasco’s Career

After completing his university, Femi joined the National Troupe of Nigeria as a Music Instructor, under the Ministry of Information and Culture. But after some time, he left his government job to pursue his Business in music. And then started to work as a musicologist and worked the same for the next 30 years.
