What is Kyle Williams' Net Worth?

Kyle Williams is an American former professional football player who has a net worth of $22 million. Kyle Williams was born in Ruston, Louisiana in June 1983. He is a defensive tackle who played at Ruston High School. Williams played his college football at LSU where he won the BCS national championship in 2003. He was first-team All-SEC and second-team All-American in 2005. Kyle Williams was drafted #134 overall by the Buffalo Bills in the 2006 NFL Draft.

He was second-team All-Pro in 2010. Williams was selected to the Pro Bowl in 2010, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2016, and 2018. He played his entire 13 season career with the Bills and had 610 tackles, 48.5 sacks, one interception, four forced fumbles, and six fumble recoveries.  Williams signed a three year deal in 2006 for $1.8 million and a three year deal for $14.5 million in 2008.
