Gilbert Goons Gang Members Name: Arrest Charge And Violence Video

Gilbert Goons gang members have attacked many teenagers, creating an unsafe environment in the southeast valley.

Gilbert Goons is a gang of 20 or more, primarily white and upper-middle-class teenagers from several Southeast Valley High Schools.

They are known for committing attacks on teenagers in the southeast valley, especially in the Gilbert, Queen Clerk, San Tan Valley, and Chandler communities.

Besides attacks, they have also posted photos and videos of their crimes on social media.

Today, we will learn more about Gilbert Goons gang members and the crimes they have committed so far.

Gilbert Goons Gang Members Names

The crimes and attacks of this gang are increasing day by day in Southeast Valley. So, many people are curious to know about Gilbert Goons gang members and their names.

The number of Gilbert Goons gang members is 20 or even more, as described by the residents. They are also known as Gilbert Goonies or Goonies. 

The names of all the gang members are not known. But during their brutal attacks on innocent teenagers, some have been arrested.

20-year-old Jacob Penington of Gilbert, 18-year-old Christopher Fantastic, and 18-year-old Aris Arrensondo are the names of the people who were recently arrested for their attacks on innocent teenagers.

The investigation is actively ongoing to find other Gilbert Goons gang members, as there have been many attacks reported in the past few weeks.

Gilbert Goons Gang Members: Arrest Charge and Violence Video

The rise of the Gilbert Goons gang and their attack on children and teenagers are increasing day by day.

Attacks and beatings by Gilbert Goons teenagers occurred in parks, parties, and fast food restaurants.

They hit them, kicked them, beat them with brass knuckles. One boy was also admitted to a hospital in 2022 because of their brutal attacks.

At least four attacks have happened at the Gilbert In-N-Out Burger at San Tan Village Parkway and East Williams Field Road. They attack the kids for no reason and also post the videos on social media.

The parents and students said that there is the involvement of Gilbert Goons members in the death of 16-year-old Preston Lord. He was fatally beaten on 28 October.

As per sources, two adults and two juveniles have been arrested separately by The Pinal County Sheriff’s Office and Gilbert Police.

It has also been reported that five people were arrested after they were accused of involvement in gang beatings. One of the people has also admitted that he is a member of Gilbert Goons. 

The investigation is going to find the rest of the people involved in the fatal attacks.

The Gilbert Goons have also shared the videos and photos of their attacks on social media platforms like Instagram and TikTok.

There are videos where Gilbert Goons can be seen flashing handguns, handling automatic weapons, and showing off bundles of cash and drugs.

Furthermore, there is a video where Goons have recorded themselves racing on highways in high-performance vehicles and sometimes with their weapons.

The family and victim’s parents have raised their voices against the brutal attacks of Gilbert Goons.

Lastly, the police department is doing its best to find the attackers and the members involved in Gilbert Goons gangs. Later, the police might reveal more information about the incident.
