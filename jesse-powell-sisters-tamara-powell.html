Jesse Powell sisters: Tamara Powell

Jesse Powell is an R&B vocalist from Gary, Indiana. In March 1996, he issued his self-titled debut album on Silas Records/MCA. He’s the brother to Tamara Powell.

Jesse Powell sisters: Tamara Powell

Tamara Powell is Jesse Powell’s sister. She is a successful R&B/soul singer and songwriter who is best known for being a former member of the duo Trina & Tamara. She is from Gary, Indiana.

The vocalist afterwards joined EMI Music Publishing as a staff writer and is now a well-known voice producer in the business after being honored with a Grammy in 2007. It was given in the category of Best R&B Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocals and recognized her work on the song Family Affair alongside John Legend, Joss Stone, and Van Hunt.

Dr. Dre, Boyz II Men, Fantasia, Kanye West, John Legend, Eminem, Will Smith, Lauryn Hill, Jermaine Dupri, and a host of others are among the musicians with whom she worked on music projects with. In addition to funding and managing branding projects, Tamara is now a content creation executive
