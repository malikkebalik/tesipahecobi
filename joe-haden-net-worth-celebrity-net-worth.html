What is Joe Haden's Net Worth?

Joe Haden is an American professional football player who has a net worth of $20 million. Joe Haden was born in Fort Washington, Maryland in April 1989. He is a cornerback who played at Fort Washington Friendly High School. Haden played his college football for Florida and was named Sporting News National Defensive Player of the Year, Unanimous All-American, and First-team All-SEC in 2009.

He also won the BCS National Championship in 2009 and the SEC Championship in 2008. Haden was drafted #7 in the 2010 NFL Draft by the Cleveland Browns and has spent his entire career with the Browns. He was a Pro-Bowl selection in 2013 and 2014 and a Second-team All-Pro in 2013. Haden signed a five year deal for $50 million in 2010 and another five year deal for $68 million in 2014.
