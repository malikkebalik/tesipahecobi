What is Emma Willis' Net Worth?

Emma Willis is an English model and television personality who has a net worth of $8 million. She is best known for her television and radio work in the UK on networks like Channel 5, BBC, ITV and Heart FM. She has been a presenter for the hit reality series Big Brother since 2013 and started working for the franchise while presenting the spin off show, Big Brother's Bit on the Side, in 2011. She also hosts the Sunday breakfast show on Heart FM with Stephen Mulhern.
