Avani Gregg Net Worth | Celebrity Net Worth

What is Avani Gregg's Net Worth?

Avani Gregg is an American social media personality who has a net worth of $4 million. Avani Gregg earned her fortune by posting beauty and makeup videos on TikTok. In 2019, in her first TikTok to go viral, she transformed into a clown reminiscent of the DC Comics character Harley Quinn. Elsewhere online, Gregg acted in the web series "Chicken Girls" and hosts the talk show "Here For It." In 2019 Avani joined the social group The Hype House along with TikTok creators including Charli D'Amelio, Chase Hudson, and Daisy Keech.

Early Life and Education

Avani Gregg was born on November 23, 2002 in Brownsburg, Indiana. She is of Indian, Mongolian, and African-American ancestry, and has two sisters named Shanti and Priya. Growing up, Gregg was a competitive gymnast until she suffered a stress fracture in the summer of 2019, forcing her to retire from the sport. She graduated from high school early after taking classes over the summer.

TikTok

In 2019, Gregg launched her TikTok account. She soon went viral for a video showing her putting on makeup to look like a clown reminiscent of the DC Comics character Harley Quinn. This led viewers to give her the nickname "Clown Girl." At the end of 2019, Gregg joined the newly formed Hype House, a collective of teenage TikTok personalities. Originally based in Los Angeles, California, the Hype House location eventually moved to Moorpark in Ventura County. Gregg is joined in the collective by her best friend Charli D'Amelio. Since starting her TikTok account, Gregg has become known for her elaborate beauty and makeup videos. In 2020, she won the Shorty Award for TikToker of the Year.

(Photo by Alberto E. Rodriguez/Getty)

Other Online Appearances 

Beyond TikTok, Gregg acted in the YouTube web series "Chicken Girls," which was produced by Brat TV and launched in 2017 on YouTube. The series follows middle school student Rhyme McAdams and her friends as they navigate dancing, friendship, crushes, and the various vagaries of growing up. In "Chicken Girls," Gregg played the role of Gemma in season six. Other cast members include Annie LeBlanc, Hayley LeBlanc, Hayden Summerall, Brooke Elizabeth Butler, Riley Lewis, Mads Lewis, and Indiana Massara.

In late 2020, Gregg began hosting her own talk show, "Here For It." Broadcast on Facebook Watch, the program features Gregg helping her Gen-Z fans with a variety of issues affecting their lives.

Fashion Shop

Gregg has a resale fashion shop called That's Avani's. Products include hoodies, heels, dresses, beanies, sweaters, and more. She also partnered with Skechers to create fashion sneakers.

Personal Life

Gregg previously dated social media personality Anthony Reeves, whom she had first met through TikTok's predecessor, Musical.ly. The pair broke up in late 2022 amid unsubstantiated rumors of Reeves's infidelity. Subsequently, Gregg began a relationship with fellow TikToker Quewin Maritz.

In 2020, Gregg announced that she had an upcoming memoir that would be published by Gallery Books.
