What is Robert Webb's Net Worth?

Robert Webb is an English actor, comedian, and writer who has a net worth of $4 million. Robert Webb was born in Boston, Lincolnshire, England in September 1972. He is best known for being one half of the double act Mitchell and Webb. He attended Robinson College and became vice-president of the theater club the Footlights. Webb met Mitchell at a Cinderella audition in 1993. Mitchell and Webb put together their first project in 1995. The comedy double act is best known for starring in the TV series Peep Show and their sketch show That Mitchell and Webb Look. As an actor Robert Webb has starred in the TV series Bruiser in 2000.

He starred as Robin in the series The Smoking Room from 2004 to 2005. In 2005 he starred as Bill Hathaway in the TV series Blessed. From 2011 to 2012 he had a recurring role as Jedrington Secret-Past in the series The Bleak Old Shop of Stuff. From 2011 to 2012 he starred as Dan in the TV series Fresh Meat. In 2013 he starred as Neil Tilly in the TV mini-series Ambassadors. He has also starred in the films Confetti, Magicians, and The Wedding Video. Webb won a BAFTA TV Award for Best Comedy Programme for That Mitchell and Webb Look in 2009.
