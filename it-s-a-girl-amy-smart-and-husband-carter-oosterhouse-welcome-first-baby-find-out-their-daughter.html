It’s a girl!

Amy Smart and her TV star husband, Carter Oosterhouse, have welcomed their first baby, a daughter named Flora.

The 40-year-old actress announced their little girl’s arrival and shared the first photo of baby Flora — whose pretty name means “flower” in Latin — via her Instagram page on Friday, Dec. 31.

“It is with great gratitude and pleasure to welcome our little girl Flora ? to the world. What a blessed way to bring in 2017?? Amen and Thank You God for this special new life!! ????,” Amy captioned a cute photo of herself posing with Carter, also 40, and their newborn daughter.

“We are so excited to announce the birth of our little lady… Flora! We love you more than you know and can’t wait to experience the journey. What a blessing,” Carter captioned the same family photo on his Instagram page.

Amy’s fans were eager to send their well wishes to the couple via social media following Flora’s birth. “Welcome to mommyhood! Congratulations to you and your husband!” one commenter wrote on the Just Friends star’s Instagram photo.

Amy and Carter — who works as a TV carpenter and currently hosts three HGTV shows, Carter Can, Red Hot & Green, and Million Dollar Room — announced their engagement after one year of dating in April 2011.

Amy and Carter in November 2015.
