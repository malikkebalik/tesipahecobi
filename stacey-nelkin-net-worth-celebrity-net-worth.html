What is Stacey Nelkin's Net Worth?

Stacey Nelkin is an American actress who has a net worth of $700 thousand dollars. Stacey Nelkin was born in New York City, New York in September 1959. Nelkin made her acting debut in 1978 appearing in the television series CHiPs, Eight is Enough, Fish, The Paper Chase, and The Waltons. In 1979 she made her film debut in the movie California Dreaming. Nelkin also starred in the movies Serial, Up the Academy, Going Ape!, Halloween III: Season of the Witch, Yellowbeard, Get Crazy, Distant Cousins, Bullets Over Broadway, and Everything Relative.

Stacey also appeared in episodes of The Chisholms, Trapper John, M.D., Walt Disney's Wonderful World of Color, Simon & Simon, The A-Team, Murder, She Wrote, The Twilight Zone, 1st & Ten: The Championship, and Hunter. One of her known roles came as Christy Russell on the soap opera Generations in 1990. Nelkin dated Woody Allen and claims that his film Manhattan was based on their romantic relationship. She is a relationship expert who has her own website and YouTube channel and wrote the book You Can't Afford to Break Up: How an Empty Wallet and a Dirty Mind Can Save Your Relationship.
