Who Is Pete Davidson?(UPDATED 2022) Career, Net Worth &amp; Dating History

Peter Davidson’s projected net worth in 2022 is around $6 million. Peter “Pete” Davidson is a New York-based American comedian and actor. Davidson is a member of the cast of Saturday Night Live. Additionally, he has been in the MTV series Guy Code, Wild’ n Out, and Philosophy.

He has appeared as a guest star on Brooklyn Nine-Nine and done stand-up comedy on Adam DeVine’s House Party, Jimmy Kimmel Live!, and Comedy Underground with Dave Attell.

Peter Davidson Early Life

Peter Davidson was born in New York on November 16, 1993. The parents of Davidson are Amy and Scott Matthew Davidson. Davidson was reared Catholic; his father was primarily Jewish, and his mother is of Irish descent.

His father was a New York City fireman who perished in the line of duty during the September 11 terrorist attacks; he was last seen going up the Marriott World Trade Center steps seconds before it fell.

Peter Davidson Career

The third episode of the MTV comedy series ‘Philosophy’ marked the beginning of Davidson’s career. The following month, he made his first of four appearances in “PDA and Moms,” the third-season episode of the MTV2 reality TV comedy series Guy Code.

In 2014, he joined the cast of Saturday Night Live with the launch of the show’s 40th season. He was the first SNL cast member born in the 1990s and one of the youngest cast members ever at 20.

In January 2019, it was reported that Pete Davidson and John Mulaney would perform a limited series of comedy concerts dubbed “Sundays with Pete & John” in New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, and Massachusetts.

Mulaney and Davidson have developed a strong relationship after performing on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon and Saturday Night Live together.
