What is Andrew Walker's Net Worth?

Andrew Walker is a Canadian actor and film producer who has a net worth of $2 million. Andrew Walker was born in Montreal, Quebec, Canada in June 1979. From 1997 to 1999 he starred as JJ in the television series Student Bodies. From 1999 to 2001 he starred as Blaire Resnickie in the TV series Radio Active. Walker starred as Rick Stage in the series Maybe It's Me from 2001 to 2002. From 2002 to 2003 he starred as Cole Harper in the television series Sabrina, the Teenage Witch. In 2011 Walker starred as John Brody in the TV series Against the Wall. He has also appeared in episodes of the TV series NYPD Blue, Reba, ER, The Big Bang Theory, CSI: NY, CSI: Miami, and Without a Trace.

In recent years Andrew has made a career of appearing in numerous Lifetime television films.
