What is Allan Loeb's Net Worth?

Allan Loeb is an American screenwriter and producer who has a net worth of $20 million. Allan Loeb was born in Highland Park, Illinois in July 1969. He created, executive produced, and wrote the television series New Amsterdam in 2008. Loeb served as executive producer for the TV series The Beast in 2009. He wrote and produced the film Things We Lost in the Fire in 2007 and wrote the film 21 in 2008.

Loeb wrote and produced the movie The Switch in 2010 and wrote the film Wall Street: Money Never Sleeps in 2010. He wrote for the movies The Dilemma in 2011, Just Go with It in 2011, Rock of Ages in 2012, and Here Comes the Boom in 2012. Loeb wrote and produced the movie So Undercover in 2013 and produced the film The Abandoned in 2015. He wrote and produced the movies The Space Between Us and Collateral Beauty in 2016. Loeb also starred in the 2004 film Surviving Eden.
