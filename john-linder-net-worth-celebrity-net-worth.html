What is John Linder's Net Worth?

John Linder is an American politician who has a net worth of $8 million. Born John Elmer Linder on September 9, 1942, in Deer River, Minnesota, he was a member of the U.S. House of Representatives from 1993 to 2011. The district that he represented at the U.S. House of Representatives was numbered the 4th from 1993 to 1997, the11th from 1997 to 2003, and the 7th from 2003 until 2011.

This Republican Party member announced that he would retire from Congress at the end of the 111th Congress. Having acquired his higher educated at the University of Minnesota Duluth, he also served in the United States Air Force, was a dentist, businessman and president of a lending institution, as well as a member of the Georgia House of Representatives.
