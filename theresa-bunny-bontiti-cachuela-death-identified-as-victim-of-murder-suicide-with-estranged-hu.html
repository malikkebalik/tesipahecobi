Who was Theresa Cachuela, often known as “Bunny Bontiti”?

Theresa Cachuela, also known as Bunny Bontiti, was a highly important figure in the realm of social media and a successful businesswoman in the beauty industry. She was most commonly referred to by her nickname.

By the time she was 33 years old, she had already made a big influence in her field, and she had inspired many people with her inventiveness and determination. In addition, she was a devoted mother to three children, one of whom was unfortunate enough to have witnessed the awful event.

In reality, what took place with the bunny bonetiti?

Theresa was having a meeting with her mother, Lucita Ani-Nihoa, close to Pearlridge Center on the day that would prove to be tragic. It was in this location that her former hubby, Jason Cachuela, who was 44 years old at the time, showed up and shot her. Following that, he ran away from the scene, leaving behind a trail of destruction in his wake.

The terrible murder of Theresa Cachuela serves as a reminder of the evils that are associated with domestic violence. A temporary restraining order was issued against Jason Cachuela because he had constantly threatened to kill himself and harassed Theresa prior to the murder. This led Theresa to seek legal action against him. The fatal shooting was carried out by Jason Cachuela in spite of these precautions and an agreement that was struck during a court hearing that he would keep a certain distance from her while maintaining visiting privileges with their children.

Body of Jason Cachuela Discovered

In a disheartening turn of events, Jason Cachuela was discovered dead in Waipahu, having committed suicide. This revelation marked the conclusion of a tragic chain of events that resulted in the deaths of two people and resulted in the absence of their parents for three generations of children.
